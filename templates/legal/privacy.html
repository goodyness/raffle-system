1. Information We Collect

We believe in transparent data collection. We collect only what is strictly necessary to operate the platform securely and legally.

  • Account Details: Email address, secured password hashes, and user roles (organizer or participant).
  • Financial Records: Transaction history, ticket purchases, deposits, and withdrawal records. Note: We do not store full credit card details on our servers; payments are processed securely by our partners (Paystack & Flutterwave).
  • Security Logs: IP addresses and device information relative to logins and critical account actions to prevent unauthorized access.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is primarily used to provide you with a fair and functional service:

  • Processing your ticket purchases and distributing winnings.
  • Verifying your identity to send you One-Time Passwords (OTPs) and confirm sensitive actions.
  • Running the provably fair winner selection algorithms accurately against eligible tickets.
  • Communicating updates about campaigns you've joined, withdrawal statuses, or critical security notices.

3. Data Security & Integrity

Commitment to Security

We prioritize the security of both your personal data and your funds. Our platform is hardened against common web vulnerabilities, utilizing atomic database transactions to guarantee financial integrity and rate-limiting to prevent brute-force attacks. However, no digital system is entirely infallible, and you must protect your login credentials.

4. Information Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties. We may share necessary information with:

  • Payment Processors: For the sole purpose of clearing payments and processing your withdrawals.
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law, subpoena, or to prevent fraudulent or illegal activity.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use essential cookies strictly to maintain your session (keeping you logged in) and to implement security mechanisms (like CSRF protection). We do not deploy invasive third-party tracking scripts across our core service pages.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant material changes, we will notify you either through the email address you have provided or by placing a prominent notice on our platform.

If you have any questions regarding how we handle your data, please contact our privacy team.
